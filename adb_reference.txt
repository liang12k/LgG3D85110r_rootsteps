
ADB: Android Debug Bridge

for adb, get the latest version.

  as of Android 4.2.2, there's a RSA enforcement and needs update on adb
  see: http://forum.xda-developers.com/galaxy-nexus/help/resolved-adb-longer-android-4-2-2-t2144709

  -have at least adb version 1.0.31
   >version 1.0.32 was used by following XDA guide from simmac
    http://forum.xda-developers.com/android/general/adb-fastboot-binaries-os-x-inclusive-t2941205


update Android SDK:
http://developer.android.com/sdk/index.html


when doing cmd 'adb push', the cursor blinks (on mac terminal)
-there isn't a feature to display the status
-confirmed: pushed PacMan rom and gapps w/o any output until it completed
see XDA post by runganwt:
http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?t=2183023&page=1875


easily reboot into recovery using cmd:
adb reboot recovery
see link: http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?t=1853159
